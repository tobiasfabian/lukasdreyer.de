Text: 

F​REIRAUM​ S​YNDIKAT​ The ensemble was founded in 2015 based on the idea of creating create something new by combining different musical traditions and inspirations. There is already a demand internationally because of its open and unique combination of instruments and specialists.
With this project, Lukas Dreyer, the founder, has created free space for new musical encounters, which ranges from arrangement to improvisation all the way to the possibility of discovering entirely new genres.
